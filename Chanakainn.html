About Us

Chanaka Inn home stay was began in 2018. Initially comprised of just three bedrooms, it soon grew to an seven-bedroom guesthouse and paved the way for everything else that was to come.
There are seven rooms in the guest house, four of those are have attached bathrooms. It has a big kitchen where you can cook food. There is a large living room with a TV and a sofa.There are two large secure parking for you to park your vehicles. CCTV cameras on all four sides.

In the year 2018, Chanaka Inn has received a value of 8.3 points from Booking.com. So far, thousands of local and foreign tourists have come here and stayed.
We have received good comments and reviews from them.

Our guest house has special traveling packages for tourists. Click the packages and Traveling places for more details.
